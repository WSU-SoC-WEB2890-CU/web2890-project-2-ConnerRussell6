About Us

Healey Landscape Co was founded in March 2020 by Conner Healey, Conner is a 20-year-old aspiring entrepreneur. Healey Landscape Co services the Weber County Area at the most affordable price in town.

Since the opening of our business, we’ve been committed to providing service of the highest quality, paying particular attention to working efficiently while keeping the lines of communication with our clients clear and concise.

Our mission at Healey Landscape Co is simple: to provide high-quality services in a timely manner. Our team caters to each project’s specific needs to ensure excellence. We hope you’ll find what you’re looking for. For more information or general inquiries, feel free to get in touch today.
